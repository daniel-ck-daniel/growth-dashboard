When I'm Struggling

Emergency toolkit. What helps when things are hard?

Build your list:
• Call [name]
• Go for a walk
• Read [specific passage/book]
• Do [specific practice]
• Remember [specific truth]

Make it concrete. You won't think clearly in the moment — decide now what helps.
